Privacy Protocol

DATA MINIMIZATION

NEXTBASE ONE operates on a principle of temporary session processing. We do not maintain permanent logs of user-inputted narrative data. Your privacy and intellectual footprint are protected by our multi-layered encryption gateway.

STRIPE SECURITY

Financial interactions are encapsulated within the Stripe secure vault. NEXTBASE ONE never accesses or stores your primary payment credentials. All billing is handled under PCI-compliant standards.

GLOBAL COMPLIANCE

Our protocols are designed to meet international data protection standards, ensuring that your cognitive processing remains confidential and secured from third-party interception.
