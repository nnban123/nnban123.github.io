Privacy Policy

Your Safety & Data Protection

01. DATA PROTECTION

GLB focuses on your privacy. We do not keep permanent records of the text you translate. Once your session is finished, the data is removed from our temporary processing layer. Your privacy is our priority.

02. FOR FAMILIES & CHILDREN

We believe in a safe internet. GLB does not collect personal information or build profiles of our users. It is a clean, simple tool designed for safe use by people of all ages, including children.

03. SECURE PAYMENTS

All subscription payments are handled by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card information. Your financial data stays between you and the bank.

04. NO THIRD-PARTY SHARING

We do not sell your data. We don't track you for ads. GLB exists only to help you communicate across languages safely and easily.
